Notes for Hannah (Spouse 1)
Hannah (White) Crow was separated from her husband Robert Crow when in 1842 he was transported to Van Diemens Land (modern day Tasmania) in Australia (on a convicts ship). She remained in Barnsley and remarried twice.

Hannah White's second marriage could be seen as being bigamous being only 4 years after Robert [CROW] went to Van Diemen's Land. She should have waited until 1849 as 7 years after the transportation of a convicted criminal was the relevant timescale for a legal remarriage. (RogerBean20, 28 Sep 2008, ANCESTRY.CO.UK)
