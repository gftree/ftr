Notes for Ann (Hannah) (Spouse 1)
Set further away from the main commercial core is the site of Barnsley
District General Hospital. This area broadly corresponds with the site of the former Barnsley Union Workhouse which was built in 1852 after the formation of Barnsley Poor Law Union in 1850 (Higginbotham 2000 [accessed 10/04/08]).

In the 1930s the site came under the control of West Riding
Council and became Barnsley Municipal Hospital. There was a major
rebuilding of the hospital in the late 20th century and by 1973 mapping the workhouse building had been demolished and modern hospital buildings built in its place. The hospital has completely removed the workhouse.
