Notes for Eliza (Spouse 1)
Eliza first married Thomas NUSSEY, B: 1835, D: 1873 in Barnsley. (Thomas Nussey's wife's name is missing from the FreeBMD Marriage Index - but it is Eliza).

Thomas NUSSEY died just 4 months prior to Eliza NUSSEY, who was now a widow, married John CROW. Eliza was pregnant by John CROW at the time of her second marriage. Hannah CROW was born later in 1874. (Hannah CROW was later known as Hannah MASON.)

When John CROW died in 1877, Eliza CROW was widowed for a second time. She had a long-term relationship leading 17 years later to marriage in 1896 with Thomas MASON. Their only child was William Mason CROW (B: 1879) who was born 17 years prior to her 3rd marriage when her name was still Eliza CROW (though widowed and though the father was Thomas MASON whom she eventually married 17 years later).

This explains our family connection to the name CROW. Also it explains the family connection to the name MASON.

Had Eliza been married to Thomas MASON when William Mason CROW was born he would have been called William MASON and our family name would have been MASON not CROW.
