Notes for John Crow
The Robert CROW lineage (but not his/our CROW surname) is passed on through John and Eliza's two girls under their RENNISON and HARRIS female surnames (which are not easy to trace within the last 100 years): 1) Hannah HARRIS (m) b: 1879 and her daughter Fanny BULLOCK (m) b: 1893 / 2) Sarah Ann RENNISON (m) b: 1876 and her daughter Eleanor b: 1896, and her son Thomas RENNISON b: 1898, and her son John RENNISON b: 1900. The CROW surname had been passed onto our (William Mason CROW) lineage (even though we were nearly surnamed MASON). The original CROW lineage lost the CROW surname because of the female descendants mentioned above.
