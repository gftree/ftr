Notes for John Crow
The Robert CROW lineage (but not the surname) is passed on through John and Eliza's 2 girls under the RENNISON and HARRIS female surnames (which are not easy to trace within the last 100 years):

1) Hannah HARRIS (m) b: 1879
and her daughter Fanny BULLOCK (m) b: 1893

2) Sarah Ann RENNISON (m) b: 1876
and her daughter Eleanor b: 1896
and her son Thomas RENNISON b: 1898
and her son John RENNISON b: 1900

This is how the CROW name evolved/changed in the lineage tangential to William Mason CROW from whom we are descended.
Notes for Eliza (Spouse 1)
Eliza first married Thomas NUSSEY, B: 1835, D: 1873 in Barnsley. (Thomas Nussey's wife's name is missing from the FreeBMD Marriage Index - but it is Eliza).

Thomas NUSSEY died just 4 months prior to Eliza NUSSEY, who was now a widow, married John CROW. Eliza was pregnant by John CROW at the time of her second marriage. Hannah CROW was born later in 1874. (Hannah CROW was later known as Hannah MASON). When John CROW died in 1877, Eliza CROW was widowed for a second time.

Eliza then had a long-term relationship leading 17 years later to her third marriage in 1896 with Thomas MASON.

Their only child was William Mason CROW (B: 1879) who was born 17 years prior to their marriage when her name was still Eliza CROW (though widowed and though the father was Thomas MASON whom she eventually married 17 years later). Their son William inherited Eliza’s surname CROW as she was unmarried.

Had Eliza been married to Thomas MASON when William Mason CROW was born he would have been called William MASON and our family name would have been MASON not CROW.

This explains our family connection to the name CROW. Also it explains the family connection to (and why William’s middle name was) MASON.
