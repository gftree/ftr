Introduction
These research files summarise the current research into our Yorkshire-centred Crow/Lester family tree.  At the moment the family tree includes over 300 individuals in hundreds of places with most in England (Yorkshire, Derbyshire, Suffolk, Norfolk), Ireland (Co. Tipperary, Co. Clare) and Australia (Tasmania/Van Diemen’s Land and Victoria) including convict transportation and the Gold Rush in Victoria. They go all the way back to 1686.
Visit the main website at: https://crowlesterancestry.wordpress.com
To begin the journey click on the home card. Notes, where available, can be viewed by clicking on the husband or wife on each family card. Photos and Documents can be viewed by clicking on the camera next to the husband’s or wife’s name (where available).
