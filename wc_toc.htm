We’d love to hear from others with a mutual interest in the people and topics discussed on this website.  Please drop us a line if you have additional information.

Family Cards will appear when you click on a person’s name in the Index. Click on the individual's name and the Person Sheet will appear with research notes on that individual. This view is useful because it contains sources, events, facts, census records and other records.

Tree – Click on the tree next to individual’s name and a family tree will appear.

Camera – If there are pictures available, a small camera will appear next to the individual’s name.
